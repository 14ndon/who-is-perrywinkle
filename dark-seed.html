Dark Seed

Dark Seed is the first Perrywinkle album, walking the line between madness and spiritual emergence. It parodies the New Age idea of "star seeds"–individuals who believe their souls originated from other planets, who feel they are on Earth to help humanity and the planet evolve spiritually. Inspired by Carl Jung's ideas of the shadow, it posits that in order to exhibit "star seed"-like qualities, we must first acknowledge and integrate the madness within ourselves.
